# Growza – Professional WhatsApp Reply Templates

Use these templates to respond to customers quickly and professionally. You can save these as **Quick Replies** in your WhatsApp Business app.

---

## 1. Initial Greeting (General Inquiry)
**Trigger:** When a customer sends the pre-written "interested in growing my business" message.

> "Hello! Thanks for reaching out to **Growza – Business Growth Agency** 🚀. We'd love to help you scale your business!
>
> To give you the best advice, could you please tell us:
> 1. Your Business Name?
> 2. Which service are you most interested in (Websites, Ads, SEO)?
> 
> Looking forward to working with you! – Preetham & Prajwal"

---

## 2. Website Inquiry
**Trigger:** When they ask specifically about website design.

> "Hi there! Glad you're interested in a new website 💻. At Growza, we focus on high-converting, mobile-first designs.
>
> Our packages are very affordable and result-driven. Would you like to see our portfolio or discuss a custom requirement?"

---

## 3. Ads & Marketing Inquiry
**Trigger:** When they ask about Instagram/Facebook Ads.

> "Hey! Ready to boost your sales? 📢 Our Instagram Ads strategy focuses on high ROI and targeting the right audience.
>
> Could you share your target location and what product/service you're looking to promote? Let's get those leads coming in!"

---

## 4. Professional "Busy" Reply
**Trigger:** When you receive a message after hours or while busy.

> "Hello! Thank you for contacting Growza 🚀. We've received your message and one of our experts (Preetham or Prajwal) will get back to you within the next 2 hours. 
>
> In the meantime, feel free to check out our latest work on our website!"

---

## 5. Pricing/Proposal Follow-up
**Trigger:** After sending a quote.

> "Hi! Just following up on the growth plan we discussed for your business. We're ready to start whenever you are! 📈
>
> Do you have any questions about the pricing or the process? Happy to jump on a quick call to clarify anything."
